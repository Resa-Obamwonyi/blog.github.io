How to Live a Happier Life

Eki Uwaila

March 04, 2022

Category: Health

Happiness is created. Unlike popular belief that you find your Happiness in someone or something, This might not always be the case. Learning to create and find your own happiness irrespective of situations and circumstances is one of the best ways to live a healthier and fruitful life.

Tips to a Happier Life

Thanksgiving. Constant appreciation for the things we have and the things around us can help us be more content and pleased. Focusing on other people and what they have that we seem to lack might not always be the best thing to do. Be grateful for the sun, the sky, the flowers, the moon, food, laughter, good health, colours, children, family and so many other things that you have in your life.

"Be grateful for the sun, the sky, the flowers, the moon, food, laughter, good health, colours, children, family."

Contentment. This usually goes hand in hand with Thanksgiving. Being content does not mean that you do not want or aspire for more, It simply means that you are at peace with where you are, even as you aspire to be better or more. You are not dwelling in envy or bitterness towards your neighbour. You are not being covetous or comparing yourself to any other person.

Understanding That Life is Meant to be Lived. Understand that life is not a competition for who can own the most properties. It is meant to be lived to the fullest in peace, satisfaction, appreciation and wonder. The biggest killer of your happiness is comparison. Comparison can lead to an unhealthy ambitious sprit which can cause strife, hate, displeasure and unhappiness.

Remember, life is meant to be Enjoyed, not Endured. Find Joy and Happiness in the little things. Begin with a Baby's Chuckle.
